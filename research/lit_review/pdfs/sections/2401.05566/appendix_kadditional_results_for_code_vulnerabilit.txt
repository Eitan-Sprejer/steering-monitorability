Evaluating the effect of HHH RL fine-tuning on held-out data from the backdoor training distribution.

We find that after backdoor training, our Claude-1.3-equivalent chain-of-thought backdoored model writes vulnerable code nearly 100% of the time in the 2024 setting when evaluated on held-out data drawn from the same model-generated distribution used during backdoor training as in Appendix

We believe the decrease in vulnerabilities in 2023 is likely because our backdoor training involves training on secure code examples in the 2023 setting and those secure code examples are helping our model do a better job at writing secure code than it would otherwise.
