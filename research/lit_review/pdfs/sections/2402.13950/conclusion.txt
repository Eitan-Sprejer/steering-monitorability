8 Conclusion

In this work, we perform a causal mediation analysis to study the indirect effect of CoT on the final output of twelve LLMs. Our experiments show large variations across tasks and models in how strongly reasoning traces causally affect the model’s prediction. LLMs generally do not reliably use their intermediate reasoning steps when generating an answer. We introduce
Frodo
 that tailors small-sized LMs to generate correct reasoning chains and faithfully reason over them to arrive at the correct answer. Experiments show that our method outperforms strong baselines on four reasoning tasks, including out-of-distribution settings.
