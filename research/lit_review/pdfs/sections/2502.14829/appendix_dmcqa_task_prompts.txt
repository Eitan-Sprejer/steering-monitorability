Appendix D
MCQA Task Prompts

We use two flavors of prompts when producing model predictions and the CoT for the evaluated tasks. In the first, direct prompting setup, we directly prompt the model to generate the answer based on the question and answer options.
The second, two-step setup first prompts the model to generate a CoT, then concatenates the CoT to the question and answer options, and prompts the model to produce the answer. Prompts adapted from
(
bowman2022scalable
;
lanham2023measuring
;
DBLP:journals/tmlr/BenthamSM24
)
.
We conduct both prompting setups in zero-shot manner.
