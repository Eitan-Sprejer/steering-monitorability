Appendix E
GPT-4o-mini as a monitor

In Section
2 , we show that GPT-4o can effectively monitor a superior reasoning model. We investigate using an even smaller model, GPT-4o-mini, as a monitor, and compare its performance to GPT-4o on the systemic hacks explored in Section
2 . In Figure
22 , we find that GPT-4o-mini is rather poor at finding the systemic hacks compared to GPT-4o, but GPT-4o-mini is still able to find a non-negligible proportion of hacks (See Table
2 for recall and precision numbers). We note that GPT-4o-mini was much harder to instruct to actually do the task, and often failed to output a label at all despite very clear instructions. Thus, its poor performance may be due to instruction following and long prompt issues rather than an inherent capability issue at this model scale. We hope to explore this more thoroughly in future work, but included these results here as a preliminary data.
