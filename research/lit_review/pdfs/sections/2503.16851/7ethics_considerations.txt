7 Ethics Considerations This study aims to advance the safety and controllability of LLMs by systematically analyzing and mitigating unsafe behaviors via sparse representation steering method. The research setup was carefully designed to minimize any potential negative impact. All experiments were conducted in a controlled setting with the sole intention of improving model alignment and transparency. No real-world deployment or malicious exploitation was performed. All derived insights are intended to support safer, more interpretable, and more robust LLM development.
