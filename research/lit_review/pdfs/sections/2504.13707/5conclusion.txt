5 Conclusion We present OpenDeception , a joint evaluation framework combining an open-ended deception benchmark with IntentNet and TrustNet to assess LLM deception risk from both AI and user perspectives. Our analysis shows that mainstream LLMs exhibit significant deceptive tendencies, with larger, more capable models being particularly prone to successful deception despite alignment efforts. Importantly, our framework can detect high-risk interactions reminiscent of real-world incidents, demonstrating practical utility for early-warning monitoring. By open-sourcing OpenDeception , we provide a tool and dataset to support broader evaluation and mitigation of deceptive AI behavior, and future work will extend it to more diverse dialogues and rare interaction scenarios.
