Appendix G
Embeddings model

We used
all-MiniLM-L6-v2
 with a maximum sequence length of
256 256
 tokens and a hidden dimension of
384 384
 as our sentence embeddings model from the
sentence-transformers

(Reimers & Gurevych,
2019 )
 library. We picked a cosine similarity threshold of
0.8 0.8
, which is the median similarity value between all sentence removed (i.e., original sentence) and sentence resampled pairs in our dataset.
