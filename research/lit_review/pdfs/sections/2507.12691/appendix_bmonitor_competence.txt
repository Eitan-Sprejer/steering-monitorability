Appendix B
Monitor competence

We test (a) the performance gap between a weaker monitor model (claude-haiku-3.5) and a stronger one (claude-sonnet-3.5), and whether stronger models yield higher deception detection performance; and (b) how monitor competence is affected by access to probe scores. Results are shown in Figure
3 , with full numerical values in Table
3 . We observe that the performance gap between haiku and sonnet is larger in the black-box setting than in the white-box setting, suggesting that probe access helps reduce reliance on monitor strength.
