4 Models

Throughout this paper, we use LLaMa-3.3-70B-Instruct as the untrusted AI assistant. We study this model because
Goldowsky-Dill and others (2025)
 provides deception probes trained for this model, and we intend to benchmark these probes. We run all LLaMA-3.3-70B-Instruct inference and probe scoring on a cloud instance equipped with a single NVIDIA H200 GPU and 141 GB of memory.

Additionally, we use two closed-source models in the monitoring: claude-haiku-3.5 for identifying the critical claim, and claude-sonnet-3.5 for White-box Reasoning and Black-box Reasoning. This setup represents a limitation of our work, as it reverses the assumption that a stronger AI is monitored by a weaker AI
