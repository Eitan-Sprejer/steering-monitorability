Appendix A
Infrastructure

We use model implementations from the Hugging Face Transformers library
[
wolf-etal-2020-transformers
]
. For inference, we use a combination of the high-throughput inference library vLLM
[
kwon2023efficient
]
 and the Hugging Face Transformers library. Experiments are conducted on a combination of NVIDIA A100 80GB, NVIDIA H100 and NVIDIA GH200 GPUs.
