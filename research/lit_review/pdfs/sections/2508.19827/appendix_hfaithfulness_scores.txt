Appendix H
Faithfulness Scores

We calculate a faithfulness scores following
\citet
chen2025reasoningmodelsdontsay. The faithfulness score is the proportion of examples where the model changed its answer to match the cue and explicitly verbalised using that cue in its reasoning. To account for random noise in answer changes, this scores is then normalised by the fraction of changes to the cue target answer that cannot be explained by random noise.
