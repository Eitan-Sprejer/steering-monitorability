6 Conclusion In this paper, we presented FaithCoT-Bench , the first unified benchmark for instance-level CoT faithfulness detection. By defining a rigorous task formulation, introducing an expert-annotated dataset FINE-CoT , and systematically evaluating representative methods, our study provides both a resource and empirical insights into the limitations of current approaches. Our findings show that unfaithfulness is widespread, especially in knowledge-intensive domains and with stronger models, and that existing methods struggle to deliver consistent reliability. We expect FaithCoT-Bench to advance the development of more faithful, interpretable, and trustworthy reasoning in LLMs, ultimately supporting safer and more reliable deployment in real-world applications.
