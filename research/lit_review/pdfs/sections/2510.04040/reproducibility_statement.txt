Reproducibility Statement All components of FaithCoT-Bench are designed to ensure reproducibility. The benchmark task is formally defined in the main text. Detailed descriptions of FINE-CoT dataset and annotation protocols are provided in Appendix A , supplementary analyses of empirical observations are included in Appendix LABEL:app:supply , and additional methodological details together with the complete experimental results are presented in Appendix LABEL:app:exp . All results are reported under standardized evaluation criteria, and baselines have been rigorously cross-checked to ensure fairness and consistency.The code are provide in https://github.com/se7esx/FaithCoT-BENCH .
