Appendix E
What Hardware Do We Use?

Using a single L40 GPU, generating responses and gathering activations using all 4 response strategies for a single behaviour and domain took an average of 1̃ hour for Llama-3.2-3B-Instruct and 1̃.75 hours for Ministral-8B-Instruct-2410. The hyperparameter search and training for probes then took a further 1̃5 minutes for linear probes and 4̃0 minutes for attention probes.
