Ethics To the extent that our work highlights a weakness in contemporary LRMs—namely, a tendency to misrepresent the nature of their reasoning processes—it is conceivable that it would also increase the likelihood of this weakness being exploited for malicious purposes (e.g. for certain forms of prompt injection attacks). Prior research has, of course, already emphasized other ways in which LRM CoTs may be unfaithful, but we still view this as a possible ethical hazard of our work.
