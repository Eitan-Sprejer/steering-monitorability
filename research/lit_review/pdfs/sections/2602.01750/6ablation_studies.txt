6 Ablation Studies We examine how Auditor capacity affects detection and mitigation by varying model size from 5M to 85M parameters. Different exploitation types require different capacities: length bias saturates at 25M, sycophancy at 35M, and code gaming at 50M—reflecting increasing complexity from surface-level features to subtle test-manipulation patterns. Beyond these optimal points, larger Auditors provide marginal improvements (details in Appendix A.2.1 ).
