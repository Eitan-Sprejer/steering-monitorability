Impact Statement This work contributes to the understanding of AI safety by studying the monitorability of reasoning in large language models. Improved monitorability is a step toward safer and more auditable AI systems. Our study focuses on characterizing emergent training dynamics, which may inform the design of more robust oversight mechanisms. A potential risk is that a better understanding of monitorability could also inform efforts to game oversight systems; however, we believe the benefits of transparent research outweigh this concern. We do not anticipate any other immediate negative societal consequences from this work.
