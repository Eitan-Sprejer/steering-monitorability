6 Conclusion

We introduced NLDD, a metric that quantifies the causal influence of reasoning chains on model predictions. Unlike accuracy-based evaluation, NLDD reveals whether models genuinely depend on their generated reasoning or merely produce post-hoc rationalizations.

Our analysis across three models and three reasoning tasks yields three findings. First, different architectures exhibit different faithfulness patterns on identical tasks. Some models (Llama, DeepSeek) exhibit causal dependence on CoT, while others (Gemma) achieve high accuracy despite negative NLDD.
