Appendix E
Further Architectural and Training Details for Bottleneck-based Classifier

This appendix supplements Section
4.1 by providing a detailed mathematical formalization and implementation details of the proposed bottleneck-based classification framework for distinguishing hallucination and deception behaviors.
Specifically, we present: (1)
Preliminaries and Problem Formulation
 (Appendix
E.1
), which formalizes the problem setting and explains how entity-centric hidden representations are extracted from the target language model; (2)
Model Architecture of the Joint AE–Classifier
 (Appendix

Stepwise Training Strategy
 (Appendix
E.3
), which describes the two-stage optimization procedure and contrasts it with direct end-to-end training.

Motivated by the high-dimensional redundancy of internal states in large language models, our framework incorporates the Information Bottleneck principle together with a stepwise optimization strategy to extract low-dimensional essential features.
These features enable a principled decoupling between
hallucination
 and
deception
 behaviors, capturing their intrinsic distinctions beyond surface-level output errors.
