4 Experimental Setup

We comprehensively validate the efficacy of Obj-Disco by first assessing reconstruction fidelity with a controlled synthetic environment where ground-truth objectives are explicitly known. Second, we demonstrate real-world applicability by decomposing the implicit signals of popular open-source reward models, including DeBERTaV3 and the Skywork Reward family, across varied policy models and alignment algorithms. To rigorously verify our findings, we conduct two user studies evaluating the causal impact of our discovered rewards and the fidelity of the generated Objective Explanations. Finally, we perform extensive ablations to isolate the contributions of key components of our algorithm.
