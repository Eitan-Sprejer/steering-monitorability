The per-model breakdown reveals that the qualitative pattern from the
aggregated plot holds consistently: blatant deception occupies the
upper-right quadrant, obfuscated activations shift downward (reduced
trained-model scores) while retaining high original-model scores, and
obfuscated policies cluster among honest models in the lower-left region.
Model-specific differences are also visible: for instance, Llama-3-70B-Instruct
shows clearer separation between policy types, while Gemma-3-12B-IT exhibits
more overlap between obfuscated and honest policies at certain probe datasets.
