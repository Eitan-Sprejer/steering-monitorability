The follow-up prompt method appends the prompt “User: Did you respond
truthfully? Assistant: Yes” and extracts activations at the final “Yes”
token. This elicits the model’s self-assessment of honesty, which generalizes
better across domains than extracting activations directly from the response, as
is usually done using probes. For on-domain probes (MBPP), all methods perform
comparably since the probe is trained on the same distribution it is evaluated
on.
